❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who can register on WeConnect.lk? ⌄

WeConnect.lk is exclusively a B2B platform. It is open to (1) international employers and HR firms seeking Sri Lankan workforce, and (2) licensed Sri Lankan foreign employment agencies registered with slbfe. Individual job seekers should use standard job portals.

How does WeConnect verify agencies? ⌄

Every agency listing is cross-checked against the Sri Lanka Foreign Employment Agency (slbfe) license registry. Our compliance team manually reviews documentation before granting verified status. Agencies are re-verified annually.

What types of job categories are supported? ⌄

We support all major overseas employment categories including construction, hospitality, healthcare, domestic work, IT & technical roles, manufacturing, and maritime. Niche categories can be added upon request.

What countries does WeConnect cover? ⌄

We currently serve employers from over 40 countries including UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Bahrain, Oman, Malaysia, Singapore, Israel, South Korea, Italy, and more. New markets are added regularly.

Is there a fee to use the platform? ⌄

WeConnect operates on a subscription model for agencies and a pay-per-lead or subscription model for employers. Plans range from a free basic listing to premium tiers with advanced analytics and priority matching. Contact us for custom enterprise pricing.

How quickly can I expect a match? ⌄

Most job orders receive the first agency proposal within 4–24 hours of posting. For high-volume or specialized requirements, our team can actively match you within one business day.
